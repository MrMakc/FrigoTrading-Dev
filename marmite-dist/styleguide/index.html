Digital Styleguide

The following Digital Styleguide regards the redesign of the website Marmite designed and produced by acti.
It aims to define the design principles used and the "User Interface" workflow for designers and developpers for future graphic declinations and the technical production of the website.


A Maintainable Style Guide as a foundation stone

At acti we think that maintaining a UI Style Guide all over the process and to make it an integral part of our development workflow is important for delivering a solid and consistent product.

The document gives and follows the good code-engineering practices used in the project to guarantee a consistent code throughout the project allowing easy maintenance of the website over time.
